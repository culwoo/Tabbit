# Tabbit Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

Effective date: 2026-04-18

Tabbit is a habit certification app operated by cullwoo. Tabbit helps users certify small daily habits with friends, manage personal and group tags, and share unlocked group story cards.

## Information We Collect

Account information:

- Google sign-in identifier and basic profile information needed to create and maintain your account.

User content:

- Certification photos, captions, selected tags, upload timestamps, and related metadata.
- Group names, group tags, membership information, threshold settings, and chat messages.
- Story-card export metadata, such as saved/shared timestamps and export count.

Device and notification information:

- Push notification token, installation identifier, platform, and notification delivery metadata.
- Permission status needed for camera, photo library/media access, and notifications.

Diagnostics and analytics:

- During development and testing, Tabbit may log limited story-card export events and handled errors to developer logs.
- Production analytics or crash reporting tools will only be enabled after the selected provider and related data disclosure are finalized.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- sign users in and maintain their accounts
- save and show personal certifications
- share certifications to selected groups and personal spaces
- calculate group threshold progress and unlock story cards
- send in-app notifications and push notifications
- diagnose reliability issues and improve core app flows
- respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests

## Sharing

Tabbit does not sell personal information.

User content is shared only according to the user's selected personal or group targets inside Tabbit. Third-party infrastructure providers, such as Supabase, Google/Firebase, Expo, and app store platforms, may process data as needed to operate authentication, storage, notifications, builds, distribution, and related app infrastructure.

## Storage and Deletion

Certification photos are stored in private app storage and shown through app-controlled access.

Users can request account or content deletion by contacting:

- tabbit2tabbit@naver.com

When requesting deletion, please include:

- the Google account email used to sign in to Tabbit
- the sentence "I request deletion of my Tabbit account"

After receiving and verifying a deletion request, we will delete or anonymize account-related data within 30 days unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or dispute-resolution reasons.

## Permissions

Tabbit asks for:

- Camera permission to capture certification photos.
- Photo library/media permission to select certification photos and save story-card images.
- Notification permission to deliver group activity and story unlock alerts.

## Children's Privacy

Tabbit is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, when appropriate, provide additional notice in the app or through the app store listing.
